For detection the geonameid

 https://www.hebcal.com/hebcal/ Follow the link

In the category "Candle lighting times" 
Choose your city name for example (Jerusalem, Israel)

Then click create Calendar

In the address bar (URL), look for the geonameid number (the city of Jerusalem, for example, will appear as the number 281184)

The number that appears to you must be filled in the geonameid field
